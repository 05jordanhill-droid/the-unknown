Cosmic Identity

A Cosmic Identity can be used in spells but are rarely actually used. The most likely to use one is a Witch, or a Blood Hunter.

Embodiments, Deities, and Titans do not require the Cosmic Identity system since a specific name is bound to them as a sign of their sheer power.

Dimensional Anchor:

Identifies the dimensional nature of the entity:

2D (Flat surfaces, e.g., shadows).

3D (Standard physical existence).

4D (Outside our 3-Dimensional space).

Distinct Mother-Timeline (Optional since only The Hunter can track this):

Specifies the overarching timeline of origin, if applicable:

T1 (Default shared timeline in the ORC Reality).

T2, T3, etc., for alternative branches.

Orbiting Dimension:

The dimension where the entity resides:

Reality (Primary ORC dimension).

X-Dimension (Neighboring side dimensions).

Existence (Outside defined dimensions).

Era:

Historical era of birth or existence within Reality:

The Beginning: 1

Age of Chaos: 2

Age of Order: 3

Age of War: 4

Age of Deities: 5

Age of Balance: 6

Age of Imbalance: 7

Age of Titans: 8

Numbered Bullet Point:

A major numbered event within the specified Era that serves as a historical timestamp anchor.

Is categorized in Miscellaneous History in bullet points.

Example: The beginning of a great war or the rise of a deity.

Year of Birth:

Defined by the ORC calendar:

100 days per year, based on the artificial day-night system.

Day:

Specific day within the year of birth.

Hour, Minute, Second:

Time of birth within the day:

30 Hours per day.

100 Minutes per hour.

100 Seconds per minute.

Minute Measurement:

A finer measurement for synchronization if required:

Precise down to fractional units of a second.
