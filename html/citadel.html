Origins

Official Numbering System

Human-like beings in this Reality have 4 fingers on each hand (A nod to the fact that their Reality is more “animated” than ours), so their number system is in base 8 rather than base 10. The best way for us to do math would be to just translate the number given in base-8 to base-10.

Even though the first Humans there had 5 fingers on each hand and used the base-10 system, their children and onward they had 4 fingers on each hand. Base-10 is present in this world due to that but only for the measurement of time, except in use for Cosmic ID in which it uses base-8. Base-10 is also represented with the same characters as we know them while they show the base-8 with unique characters to differentiate when they are used.

Official Timekeeping System

***Base-10***

20 Years = Human Adult (Approx. 19 Years old for us)

100 Days per Year

30 Hours per Day

100 Minutes per Hour

100 Seconds per Minute

The first Humans put here constructed a massive beacon and an even bigger half circle that revolves around it to create the illusion of day and night.

There are 3 parts to a full day:

1/3: Tail end of Night time and first half of Day time

2/3: Second half of Day time and tail beginning of Night time

3/3: Majority of Night time
