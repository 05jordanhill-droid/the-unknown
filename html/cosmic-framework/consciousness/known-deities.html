<Hierarchy of Consciousnesses

Arch-deities

Death (The Judge)

Domain: The afterlife, judgment, and the balance of life and death. He covers Necromancers.

Time (The Huntsman)

Domain: Time, the flow of events, and the untamed forces of nature (embodied through a face on the back of the head representing the Deity of the Wild). He covers Healers.

Truth (The Seer)

Domain: Truth, knowledge, and the unveiling of hidden secrets. She covers Seership and Oracles.

Lesser Deities

The Deity of Stories (The Puppeteer)

Domain: Storytelling, invasion of agency, narrative control, and manipulation of events through the power of story.

The Deity of Intrusion

Domain: The violation of privacy, forcing information or secrets from others, though not necessarily accurate or truthful.

The Deity of Oaths

Domain: Binding oaths, contracts, and promises—overseeing and enforcing the integrity of vows made between individuals and deities. She oversees all Paladin oaths, is aware of all Cleric bonds, and when left out is what makes a Warlock.

The Deity of Vengeance

Domain: Retribution, revenge, and the pursuit of justice or punishment. He often influences Bloodhunters.

The Deity of Transactions

Domain: Deals, exchanges, and the fine print of agreements, whether in goods, services, or power. Has a large variety of forms, personalities, and representatives to provide a wide range of deals. He is often mistaken as different Deities when in fact those multiple Deities are just him. He is responsible for most shady deals and thus most Warlocks and cults.

The Deity of the Wild

Domain: Nature, survival, untamed forces, and the primal aspects of existence. It exists on the back of Time’s head. It covers Barbarians and Rangers.

Peris, The Deity of External Perfection

Domain: Physical excellence, outward beauty, and flawless execution of actions. He covers Fighters.

Solara, The Deity of Internal Perfection

Domain: Mental clarity, emotional balance, spiritual growth, and self-mastery. She covers Monks.

The Deity of Curiosity and Unrest

Domain: Endless inquiry, exploration, and the disruption of settling. She inspires the pursuit of knowledge and change but often leaves unease and turmoil in their wake. She often inspires Artificers.
